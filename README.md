## About

### 🕌 Precise Prayer Times

- All five daily prayers plus Sunrise, Duha, Qiyam al-Layl and Tahajjud
- Live countdown to the next prayer so you're always prepared
- More than 20 internationally recognised calculation methods (Muslim World League, Umm Al-Qura, ISNA, Egyptian, and more)
- Hanafi and Shafi'i juristic schools support
- Browse past and future dates with a built-in date picker
- Hijri (Islamic) calendar date display
- 12-hour or 24-hour time format support

### 📖 The Holy Quran & Audio

- Read the entire Quran with beautiful typography and translations
- High-quality audio recitations integrated seamlessly with your iOS Control Centre
- Resume reading or listening exactly where you left off

### 💡 Daily Inspiration

- Start your day with a brand new "Ayah of the Day"
- Beautiful "Daily Dawah" (Authentic Hadiths and Islamic quotes) dynamically updated every day

### 🧭 Qibla Compass

- Built-in compass shows the precise direction to the Kaaba from your location
- Real-time bearing readout in degrees

### 📍 Flexible Location

- Automatic GPS detection
- Search for any city worldwide
- Enter coordinates manually for maximum precision and privacy

### 🍎 Deep Apple Ecosystem Integrations

- Dynamic Island & Live Activities: Watch the prayer countdown tick down live, right from your Lock Screen or Dynamic Island!
- Home & Lock Screen Widgets: Glance at upcoming prayer times without ever needing to open the app
- Apple CarPlay: Safely browse Surahs and listen to high-quality Quran recitations from your car's dashboard
- macOS Menu Bar: Keep Markaz running quietly in your Mac's menu bar for instant access while working
- Apple Watch: Beautifully optimized watchOS companion app to check times and the Qibla directly from your wrist

### 🔔 Powerful Notifications & Android Integrations

- Home Screen Widgets: View your daily prayer times at a glance right from your home screen using native Android widgets
- Android Auto: Safely browse Surahs and listen to Quran recitations directly from your car's dashboard
- Smart Alarms: Beautiful adhan sounds (Makkah, Madinah, Al-Aqsa) that can be configured to gently bypass "Do Not Disturb" mode
- Live Notifications: Keep a live, persistent countdown in your notification tray
- Wear OS: Check prayer times seamlessly straight from your wrist

### 🪟 Native Windows Experience

- System Tray Integration: Keep Markaz running quietly in the background via the Windows System Tray for instant access without cluttering your taskbar
- Seamless Media Controls: Control Quran audio playback natively using your keyboard's media keys or the Windows volume overlay
- Launch at Startup: Configure Markaz to automatically start when you turn on your PC so you never miss a prayer while working
- Beautiful Desktop UI: A fully responsive, beautifully crafted desktop interface that adapts perfectly to your monitor size and supports native Windows dark mode

### 🐧 Native Linux Experience

- Universal Distribution: Available natively on the Snap Store and Flathub, or download the universal AppImage to run seamlessly across all major distributions
- System Tray Integration: Keep Markaz running quietly in the background via your desktop's System Tray (AppIndicator support) for instant access without cluttering your dock
- Seamless Media Controls: Control Quran audio playback natively using your keyboard's media keys or your desktop environment's sound menu via MPRIS integration
- Beautiful Desktop UI: A fully responsive, beautifully crafted desktop interface that adapts perfectly to your monitor size and supports native GTK/Linux dark modes

### 🎨 Beautiful & Adaptive Design

- Dynamic animated weather backgrounds that reflect real conditions
- Dark, Light, or System-matched theme
- Fully supports multiple languages, including Right-to-Left language support
- Cloud sync your preferences seamlessly across devices
- Choose which prayers to show or hide on your dashboard
